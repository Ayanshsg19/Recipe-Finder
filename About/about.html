Website's Purpose
This website was created with the sole purpose to help people, who at the end of the day always have the question - "khaane mei kya banau ?". It takes the ingredients and raw food material you have at home but have no idea what to make from it. It then output you the best dish, which would contain most of your input ingredient (if not all) with the respective recipe, to make your hunger and thought process ("of what to make or what not to make" - reprised version of the Hamlet © ) reduced to a minimum.
What to do ?
Some steps involve, getting all the ingredients you want in your dish, have them in your mind and pour it out on the search bar provided, but don't forget to enter search button after every input. That's it, you did your part, now let us help you, let us take somethings out of your to-do lists, to lessen your worries. Within a matter of time you would have several dish which will have most of the ingredients you've entered. We will provide a link to the stepwise recipe to help you make that perfect dish.
